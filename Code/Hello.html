The Church Of QQ, The Pure One.

Just take a look at it, isn't it beautiful? - QQ

Far far away from our barren earth and greedy crowd,
There lived a deer on a mountain above the rainbow and cloud.
It always danced gracefully on the lush green grass,
Making crisp tingling sound with his feet of alloyed brass.

Its chocolaty eyes shined with cheeks like vanilla cake,
But one single drop of its tear would fill the entire lake.
If you look into its eyes you will see the mutiverse,
The vision full of life, every moment glorious.

Our people thought to bring it back
They took all guns, rope and sack.
To make the barren earth bloom again from dry,
They cut the deer's ear to make him cry.

The blood was of gold but turn into ashes on ground,
The wounded deer didn't cry but was yelling and downed.
A man came closer to the bleeding deer,
He touched the deer's back and brushed away its fear.

It's horns started glowing like the bright star on sky,
The deer closed its pearly eyes and was about to cry.
The glowing deer cried with the touch of his love,
By a single act of kindness the whole world now could be served.
